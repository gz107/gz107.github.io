# Research Projects

## Ph.D. Period

### National Key Research and Development Program, China

**Project:** Key Technology for High-Density, Large-Capacity, and Fast-Response Solid-State Hydrogen Storage  
**Role:** Studied hydrogen mass transfer in metal hydride beds and supported reactor-level performance analysis.

### National Key Research and Development Program: Intergovernmental International Science and Technology Innovation Cooperation, China

**Project:** Collaborative Research on Hydrogen Purification Materials and Process Optimization by Medium-Temperature Rapid Pressure Swing Adsorption  
**Role:** Investigated gas-mixture mass transfer in adsorption beds and contributed to adsorption-process modeling for hydrogen purification.
